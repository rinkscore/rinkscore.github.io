SECTION FOUR

The Teams

  • 🥇

    Calgary Junior Stars

    Calgary, AB

    Founded 2004 as the flagship U10 program of the Calgary Junior Hockey Association. Home rink: WinSport Arena B at Canada Olympic Park. Known for fast transitions and a deep three-line rotation.

  • 🥉

    Red Deer Cubs

    Red Deer, AB

    Established 1998 out of the Red Deer Minor Hockey Association. Home rink: Kinex Arena. Back-to-back Central Alberta U10 finalists in 2024 and 2025.

  • Kelowna Lightning

    Kelowna, BC

    Founded 2009 in the Okanagan Minor Hockey Association. Home rink: Capital News Centre. First Alberta tournament appearance — making the trip east over the Rockies for the spring classic.

  • 🥈

    Saskatoon Wolves

    Saskatoon, SK

    Established 2007 by the Saskatoon Minor Hockey Association. Home rink: ACT Arena. Won the Prairie U10 Invitational in 2025; known for a stingy defensive structure.
