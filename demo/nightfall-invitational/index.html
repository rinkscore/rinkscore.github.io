I
Programs

Six of Canada's top prep programs.

Northwood Academy Knights

Calgary, AB

Founded 2008. Three-time Western Prep Conference finalists. 14 alumni currently in NCAA Div I programs.

Stonebridge Prep Wolves

Vancouver, BC

West-coast academy program known for two-way structure. Defending Nightfall champions.

Aurora College Aviators

Aurora, ON

Host program. Aviation-themed academy founded 2014. Strong CHL pipeline.

Riverdale Academy Royals

Montreal, QC

Bilingual prep program. Five players selected in the 2025 QMJHL draft.

Westgate Prep Falcons

Winnipeg, MB

Prairie powerhouse. Reigning Manitoba Prep League regular-season title-holders.

Tidewater Academy Mariners

Halifax, NS

Maritime academy with strong USHL ties. First Nightfall appearance since 2022.
